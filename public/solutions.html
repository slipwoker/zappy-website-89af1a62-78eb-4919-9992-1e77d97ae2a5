Road and transportation infrastructure lighting

Lighting fixtures for main roads, interchanges and highways — designed for maximum light uniformity, high energy efficiency and complete resistance to extreme weather conditions. Suitable for lighting specifications of transportation authorities and municipalities.

Public area lighting

Lighting solutions for squares, parks, pedestrian paths and urban commercial areas. Models combining architectural design with precise photometric performance, while meeting international safety and lighting regulations.

Facade and outdoor architectural lighting

Lighting fixtures for wall washing, facade highlighting and architectural design of public and commercial buildings. Range of beam angles, color temperatures and IP ratings for every unique project.

Tunnel road lighting

Tunnel lighting requires unique engineering expertise for managing the optical transition between outdoor and indoor lighting. Our systems are designed to prevent glare effects and the 'black hole' phenomenon, while ensuring uniform light distribution that enhances driver safety at all hours. The lighting fixtures are manufactured from corrosion-resistant materials that withstand pollution and soot, ensuring long-term engineering durability and significant savings in maintenance and energy costs.

Industrial and urban lighting fixtures

Fixture series for industrial lighting, ports, airports and large urban complexes. Steel and aluminum construction with IP65 rating and above, suitable for environments with high exposure to dust, moisture and corrosive conditions.

Low and medium voltage electrical cables

Families of low and medium voltage electrical cable suppliers manufactured according to stringent international standards — IEC, BS and UL. Designed for distribution networks, infrastructure projects, public buildings and industrial facilities of all scales.

Solar PV Systems

In the solar energy field, the cable is the lifeline of the system. We supply dedicated DC cables meeting the stringent EN 50618 (H1Z2Z2-K) standard, designed for continuous resistance to direct UV radiation, extreme temperatures and prolonged exposure to aggressive outdoor conditions.
Our conductors are made of tinned copper to prevent corrosion and optimize electrical conductivity, ensuring minimum energy losses and maximum yield over decades. With double insulation and high resistance to mechanical wear, T.D GLOBAL cables provide the engineering resilience required for ground installations, rooftop systems and mega solar infrastructure projects. Fire-resistant electrical cables

We offer fire-resistant cabling systems designed to ensure continuity of electrical flow and data transmission even under flames and extreme temperatures. The cables meet stringent international safety standards (such as IEC 60331), are characterized by very low smoke emission and no toxic gases (LSZH), and are designed to power fire detection systems, emergency lighting and smoke extraction. This is an essential edge solution for complex public infrastructure, tunnels, medical buildings and logistics centers, where system resilience is critical for saving lives.

Electrical cable for submersible pumps

We supply electrical cables with special insulation and high durability properties, designed for continuous operation underwater and in industrial liquids. The cables offer absolute protection against moisture penetration, exceptional resistance to oils, fuels and chemicals, and immunity to chemical reactions under prolonged immersion conditions. The cable's reinforced structure ensures resistance to mechanical pressures and wear, guaranteeing continuous operation of systems in pumping wells, treatment facilities and complex infrastructure applications.
